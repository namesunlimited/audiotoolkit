Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: almost everything on AudioToolkit — every tool from Audio Converter to Noise Reducer — processes your files entirely inside your own browser. Your audio and video files are never uploaded to our servers.

1. Files You Process With Our Tools

When you use an AudioToolkit tool (Audio Converter, Trimmer, Compressor, Merge, Volume Booster, Speed Changer, Video to Audio Extractor, Audio Reverser, Fade In/Out, or Noise Reducer), the audio or video file you select is read directly by your browser and processed locally using JavaScript and the Web Audio API. It is never transmitted to AudioToolkit's servers or any third party.

2. Text to Speech and Audio to Text

Text to Speech uses your browser's or device's built-in speech engine to read text aloud locally. Audio to Text uses your browser's speech recognition feature to transcribe live microphone input; depending on your browser (for example, Chrome or Edge), this may send audio to that browser vendor's own cloud speech recognition service for processing — this is a function of the browser itself, not something AudioToolkit controls or has access to.

3. Information We Do Collect

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically log basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, for security and performance purposes. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

4. Contact Form

If you use our Contact Us form, the information you submit (name, email, and message) is used only to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

5. Cookies

AudioToolkit does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Any cookies used are strictly limited to what's necessary for the site to function.

6. Third-Party Services

Some tools load third-party JavaScript libraries (such as an MP3 encoding library) from a content delivery network (CDN) to function. These libraries run inside your browser and do not receive your files or personal data.

7. Children's Privacy

AudioToolkit is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our Contact Us page.
